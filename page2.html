YouTube Users (2023)

How Many People Are on YouTube?

YouTube is projected to reach 2.85 billion users in 2023, an increase of almost 200 million users or 9.6% growth compared to the previous year. It is the second most popular social network in the world today.

YouTube was founded in 2005 and was acquired by Google a year later. It has evolved into the world’s largest online video platform. It includes a wide range of video content created by global YouTube users, including how-to videos, educational videos, and more.

With over 500 hours of video content posted to YouTube every minute, YouTube users worldwide watch over one billion hours of video daily, generating a billion views.

YouTube ranks third among the world’s most popular video streaming and entertainment apps, with 154 million downloads from global users in 2022. It also received US $39.15 million in in-app purchase revenues from the United States and US $17.49 million from Japan.

YouTube Monthly Users and User Growth

YouTube will have around 2.85 billion users in 2023, showing a growth of 250 million users or 9.6% from the previous year. It is expected to grow by 13.68% from 2023 to 2025, or a projected 3.24 billion users.

YouTube’s global ad revenue generated US $29.24 billion in 2022, a slight increase from the previous fiscal period’s US $28.8 billion. At the same time, it accounts for 11.35% of Google’s total revenue from YouTube ads.

How Many Visits Does YouTube Receive in a Month?

With 8.4 billion visits worldwide, mobile devices generate the most YouTube traffic. Around 71.66 billion visits are generated by mobile devices, while around 8.4 billion visits come from desktop devices. In total, 80.06 billion access YouTube every month.

YouTube Premium is expected to reach 128 million subscribers worldwide in 2023, up by 48 million or 60% from the previous year. YouTube Premium is a subscription service where you can enjoy YouTube videos and YouTube music without video ads.

There are anticipated 26.7 million subscribers in the United States in 2023, accounting for 20.85% of the total subscribers globally.

YouTube Daily Active Users (DAU)

How Many People Watch YouTube Daily?

Around 368.21 million users access YouTube on Android and iOS devices daily. It is reported that 6.98 million YouTube daily active users (DAU) come from Android devices, while iOS devices account for the largest share with 361.23 million DAU.

Daily active users on YouTube who access the app from Android devices declined by 870 thousand or 11.08%, while those using iOS devices show a significant growth of 38.96% or over 100 million added DAU in just a year.

YouTube Shorts, where you can watch short video content, had 50 billion active users daily in 2022, a vast 20 billion or 66% increase from its initial release last year. They first trialed this feature in the Indian market following the ban of TikTok in the country.

Sources

Statista, Statista

YouTube User Demographics

Age and Gender Distribution of Youtube Users

In 2023, most YouTube users are 25 to 34 years old, accounting for 20.70% of total users. Males comprise 11.9% of this age group, while females comprise 8.8%. In support of this, 54.4% of YouTube users are male, while 45.6% are female.

Gen Z (ages 18-24) appears less fond of watching YouTube, ranking third in the age distribution with only 15%, lagging 1.7% behind the percentage of 35 to 44-year-olds.

Other age groups include 45 to 54 years old (11.90% of the YouTube demographics), 55 to 64 years old (8.80%), and over 65 years old (9%).

According to YouTube usage statistics in the United States, 35% of parents allow their under-11-year-old kids to watch YouTube numerous times per day, even though the online platform explicitly states that it is not recommended for users under the age of 13. In comparison, only 5% of parents encourage their children under 11 to watch YouTube videos more than once every few weeks.

Sources

Statista, Statista, Statista

Which Country Watches the Most YouTube?

India has the most significant number of YouTube users, with 467 million accessing one of the popular social media platforms in 2023. It is also worth mentioning that two Indian YouTube channels are in the top 10 of the most viewed videos worldwide.

Regarding monthly traffic to YouTube.com, the platform is visited by most internet users in the United States, with 11.7 billion visits in 2022. South Korea came in second, with 8.25 billion visits to the video-sharing and live-streaming platform. Users in India and Brazil visited YouTube.com approximately 4.2 billion and 3.6 billion times, respectively.
